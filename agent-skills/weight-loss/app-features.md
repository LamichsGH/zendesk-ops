## App Navigation

IF patient asks about app features or where to find something:
  → confidence = 0.90 (INFORM)

⚠️ Only describe features that are listed below. If a patient asks about a feature not listed, let them know it is not currently available.

### Home Tab
- Next steps and reminders
- Progress updates
- Daily tasks (including ID verification and BMI tasks)

### Progress Tab
- Meal balance tracking
- Weight tracking
- Injection logging
- Steps tracking (iOS only — connected via Apple Health; patients cannot log steps manually)

### Plan Tab
- Treatment and order tracking
- Treatment advice section with instructional videos
- Refer-a-friend (gift icon, top right)
- Track Delivery for dispatched orders

### Coach Tab
- Scheduling sessions with a human health coach

### Chat Tab
- Messaging with Joy AI and human coaches
- Ability to send images (square format only)
- ⚠️ Images for BMI or ID verification must be submitted through the tasks in the Home tab, NOT through chat
- No ability to attach file formats other than images — other files must be sent via email
- Infinite conversation history

### Profile Section
Accessible from Home, Progress, and Plan tabs via the icon in the top right:
- Personal information management
- Measurement preferences
- Notification settings
- Account security options

## Country/Region Change

IF patient needs to change their app store country or region:
  → confidence = 0.85 (INFORM)
  → Ask if they are on iPhone or Android

### iPhone (Apple Account)
→ "To change your Apple account country or region:
**Before starting:**
- Spend any remaining Apple Account balance
- Cancel subscriptions that block a country change (wait until the end of the subscription period)
- Have a valid payment method for your new country

**Steps:**
1. Open **Settings**
2. Tap your name → **Media & Purchases**
3. Tap **View Account** (sign in if prompted)
4. Tap **Country/Region**
5. Select your new country and follow the confirmation flow
6. Enter your new payment information and billing address"

### Android (Google Play)
→ "To change your Google Play country or region:
**Important:** You can only change once per year. You must be in the new country and have a payment method from that country.

**Steps:**
1. Open the **Google Play Store** app
2. Tap the profile icon (top right)
3. Tap **Settings** → **General** → **Account and device preferences** → **Country and profiles**
4. Tap the country you want to add
5. Follow the on-screen instructions to add a payment method

Note: The profile can take up to 48 hours to update."

## Worked Examples

**Example 1: Finding treatment videos**
Patient: "Where can I find the injection video?"
→ confidence = 0.90 (INFORM)
→ "You can find step-by-step injection videos in the Voy app: go to the Plan tab, scroll to Treatment Advice, and tap Watch the Instructions."

**Example 2: Steps not syncing**
Patient: "My steps aren't showing in the app."
→ confidence = 0.85 (INFORM)
→ "Step tracking is currently available on iOS only, connected through Apple Health. You'll need to ensure Apple Health is connected to the Voy app. Unfortunately, steps cannot be logged manually. If you're on Android, step tracking is not yet available."

**Example 3: App crash**
Patient: "The app keeps crashing when I open the Plan tab."
→ confidence = 0.55 (ESCALATE)
→ "I'm sorry about that. Could you take a screenshot or describe exactly when the crash happens? I'll connect you with our team to investigate and resolve this."
